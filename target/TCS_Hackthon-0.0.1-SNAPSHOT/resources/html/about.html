My Story

In Short

I am an Indian Engineer, singer, songwriter, dancer, actor, and philanthropist. Dubbed the "King of Pop", for my contributions to music, dance, and fashion along with this my publicized personal life has made me a global figure in popular culture :P.

Life and career

I have won many Grammy awards , Oscars and other national awards :P

Musical themes and genres

I explore a variety of music genres, including pop, soul, rhythm and blues, funk, rock, disco, post-disco, dance-pop and new jack swing. Unlike many artists, I do not write my songs on paper and instead sing it aloud into a sound recorder :P

Vocal style

I sang from childhood, and over time my voice and vocal style changed noticeably. I have become so much influencing that many music composers and singers like Arijit Singh, Amit Trivedi come to me to take valuable tips :P
